Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you guarantee I'll get a job? +
No — and any service that claims otherwise is not being honest with you. Hiring decisions are made by employers. What Ashley guarantees is a completed, organized service: a polished resume, verified job leads, and submitted applications.
What do I need to provide to get started? +
Fill out the intake form with your background and role preferences. For resume services, send your current resume or work history notes. For application submissions, Ashley will need your approved job list and the login email used for each portal.
Are the job leads actually remote? +
Yes. Ashley only saves and delivers verified, work-from-home opportunities with direct apply links. Every listing is scam-checked against official company websites, LinkedIn, and job domains before it ever reaches a client.
How do I pay? +
Payment is collected before work begins. Ashley accepts CashApp, PayPal, and Square. Your spot in the queue is confirmed once payment and your intake form are both received.
Can I add services after purchasing a package? +
Absolutely. Add-ons like a LinkedIn Mini Refresh, extra applications, or a Cover Letter Template can be added to any package at any time. Just reach out after your initial purchase.
